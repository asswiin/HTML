Game of Thrones,A Television series created from the Book "The Song of Ice and Fire" written by George R.R Martin

Game of Thrones is an American fantasy drama television series created by David Benioff and DB Weiss for HBO. It is an adaptation of A Song of Ice and Fire written by George R. R. Martin.GOT contian 8 seasons with 73 epsisodes.Premise and plot. Game of Thrones follows a large cast of characters and interwoven story arcs. It is primarily set on the fictional continent of Westeros, which is divided into the Seven Kingdoms and the lands in the far North beyond “the Wall,” an enormous wall of fortified ice.

House Targaryen

House Targaryen of King's Landing is a noble family of Valyrian descent who once ruled the Seven Kingdoms of Westeros.
The Targaryen sigil is a red three-headed dragon, breathing red flames on black.The house words are "Fire and Blood"
